About Jaime

Creative direction with a production mindset.

I work at the intersection of art direction, creative leadership, design systems, UX/UI, creative automation, and scalable execution — helping teams turn business needs into clear, consistent, and high-quality branded experiences.

My work sits at the intersection of creative thinking and practical execution. I help translate business needs into clear creative direction, guide teams through complex production workflows, and build systems that allow ideas to scale without losing quality, consistency, or intent.

I bring a hybrid perspective shaped by design, advertising, front-end collaboration, motion, production, and brand experience. That mix allows me to connect strategy, craft, systems, and delivery — taking creative risks while keeping the work grounded, useful, and executable.
